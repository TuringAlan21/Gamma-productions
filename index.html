Our Headquarters

New York

The main headquarters of the global technology firm, Gamma Productions, stands as a beacon of innovation in the heart of Midtown Manhattan. Located on the 48th floor of a towering skyscraper, the office commands a panoramic view of the city, from the bustling streets of Times Square to the serene expanse of Central Park. Inside, the space is a fusion of modern design and collaborative spirit, featuring open-plan workspaces, cutting-edge labs, and vibrant common areas designed to spark creativity.

Our Mission

Gamma Productions is on a mission to redefine the future of storytelling through groundbreaking technological solutions. We create intuitive and seamless digital platforms that connect people and ideas, breaking down barriers and fostering a world where collaboration knows no bounds. Our core belief is that technology should not be a tool that separates us, but a bridge that brings us closer together.
